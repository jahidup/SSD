The Legacy of Sankalp Shiksha

Sankalp Digital Pathshala was founded in **2020** in Salemgarh, Tamkuhi, Kushinagar, Uttar Pradesh. Operating under the **Sankalp Shiksha Foundation**, our vision is to provide high-quality education to children in rural and semi-urban Uttar Pradesh. What began as a local tutoring center has grown into a premium competitive coaching hub. We combine elite physical mentorship with modern AI-driven educational support.

Over the years, we have expanded our infrastructure to include smart classrooms, interactive science exhibits, computer labs, a future-tech robotics workspace, and an interactive gamezone. We maintain a strict student-to-teacher ratio, limiting offline batch sizes to 25. This ensures every student receives the guidance needed to master complex subjects and build academic confidence.

At Sankalp, we believe that academic excellence is built on daily habits, structured resources, and personalized feedback. We are committed to helping our students achieve their academic goals and succeed in their chosen pathways.

Our Core Educational Values

The guiding principles that shape our curriculum, teaching methods, and student support systems.

💡 Conceptual Mastery First

We focus on building strong foundations. Rather than rote memorization, we teach students the core principles behind every formula, enabling them to tackle unfamiliar problems with confidence.

🔧 Personalized Learning

Every student learns differently. We use weekly testing data and AI analytics to identify individual study needs, providing custom practice sheets to address specific topic areas.

🤝 Supportive Mentorship

Education is about more than just academics. Our faculty provides guidance and support, helping students manage exam pressure, build effective study habits, and prepare for future careers.

Our Historical Journey

Key milestones in the growth and development of Sankalp Digital Pathshala.

2020

Foundation & Initial Launch

Sankalp Digital Pathshala was founded under the Sankalp Shiksha Foundation in Salemgarh, Kushinagar. We began with offline preparatory classes for Class 9 and 10 board exams, working with a small group of local students to build foundational academic skills.

2022

Curriculum & Infrastructure Upgrades

We upgraded our classrooms with digital interactive displays and built fully-equipped physics and chemistry labs. We also expanded our curriculum to include dedicated preparation programs for competitive engineering (JEE) and medical (NEET) entrance exams.

2024

Integration of AI Support Tools

We integrated AI-driven support tools to assist students during self-study. We launched our 24/7 AI Doubt Solver and the Sankalp Sathi AI Mentor, allowing students to get instant answers and guidance outside of regular class hours.

2026

Regional Leadership in Education

Today, Sankalp is one of the region's leading coaching institutes, supporting over 1,500 enrolled students. We maintain a 98% board success rate and have helped hundreds of students qualify for prestigious competitive exams, continuing our mission to deliver quality education.
